Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Welcome to Toolify. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our web utilities.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google Authentication, we receive basic information from your Google Profile, such as:

  • Your Name (to personalize your dashboard)
  • Your Email Address (to identify your account)
  • Your Profile Picture (to display in the settings menu)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information solely to provide a personalized experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Local Storage & Cookies

Toolify uses local storage to remember your preferences, such as Dark Mode or Light Mode settings, so you don't have to reset them every time you visit.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and hosting. Their use of your information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You can stop our collection of your data at any time by simply logging out and disconnecting your Google account from Toolify.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer (Mohak) through the official project repository.
